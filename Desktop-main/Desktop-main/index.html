ABOUT

Sreenidhi Institute of Science and Technology (SNIST) is a prominent engineering college in Ghatkesar, Hyderabad, established in 1997 by the Sree Educational Group, known for technical education, research, and placements. Affiliated with JNTU, Hyderabad, and accredited by NAAC (A Grade) and NBA, SNIST offers B.Tech, M.Tech, and MBA programs, focusing on academic excellence, innovation, industry linkages, and global exposure.
